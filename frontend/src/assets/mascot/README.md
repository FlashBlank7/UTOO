# UTOO Mascot Assets

These mascot images depict Yutoko, the original UTOO mascot character.

- `Yutoko / 優都子 / ゆとこ` refer to the same character.
- The seven weekday PNGs use the same character, canvas size, and transparent background.
- The current v2 exports strengthen Yutoko's UTokyo-inspired atmosphere with visible ginkgo leaves, UTokyo Blue `#0B8BEE`, yellow `#FFCD00`, and an original UTOO double-ginkgo badge.
- The public sticker pack lives in `frontend/public/stickers/yutoko/` and is rendered from a shortcode whitelist.
- They are intended as the front-end fallback when Live2D is unavailable, disabled, or not yet licensed.
- The current fallback style is adult-coded and erokawaii, while staying family-safe: no nudity, no exposed intimate body parts, no transparent underwear, and no strongly sexualized pose.
- The outfits are UTokyo-inspired through abstract campus motifs only: blue/yellow accents, ginkgo details, akamon-red charms, lab badges, and student-community items.
- These assets do not include the official University of Tokyo logo, logotype, emblem, or readable university text.
- UTOO is an independent student community and is not an official University of Tokyo service.
- Third-party Live2D models and public sprite packs were used as research references only; no third-party character asset is embedded here.
- Keep future replacements tasteful, adult-coded, and readable at small UI sizes.

Local iteration archive:

- `yutoko/exports/` stores the current exported PNGs used by the app.
- `yutoko/prompts/` stores the prompt and design notes for future image iteration.
- `yutoko/prompts/yutoko-stickers-v1.md` records the first forum sticker pack.
